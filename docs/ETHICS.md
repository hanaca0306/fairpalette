# Ethics

FairPalette is not anti-AI.

The project explores whether generative AI systems can be designed with:
- creator consent
- attribution visibility
- transparent accounting
- provenance awareness
- participatory licensing structures

as first-class architectural concepts.

---

# Principles

## Opt-in Datasets
Training assets should be voluntarily provided.

---

## Creator Withdrawal Rights
Creators should be able to revoke participation and request removal of hosted assets whenever technically feasible.

---

## Transparency over Opacity
Generation systems should expose provenance and attribution metadata whenever possible.

---

## Open Standards
Attribution and provenance systems should remain interoperable and auditable.

---

## Realistic Limitations
Current AI systems cannot reliably determine exact visual origin attribution at pixel level.

FairPalette therefore treats attribution as:
- declared participation
- explicit module weighting
- logged generation structure

rather than deterministic reverse tracing.
