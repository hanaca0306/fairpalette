# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in FairPalette.

This project is currently an early-stage open infrastructure exploration focused on creator attribution, provenance, and royalty-aware AI systems.

---

# Ways to Contribute

## Development
Potential areas:
- Python SDK implementation
- diffusers integration
- attribution metadata tooling
- provenance systems
- logging infrastructure
- royalty accounting prototypes

---

## Research
Potential areas:
- creator licensing models
- AI governance
- attribution standards
- provenance metadata
- C2PA interoperability
- legal review

---

## Creator Feedback
Artists and creators are welcome to:
- critique onboarding concepts
- discuss licensing expectations
- review attribution UX concepts
- provide feedback on creator participation structures

---

# Contribution Philosophy

FairPalette prioritizes:
- transparency
- interoperability
- creator participation
- open discussion
- realistic technical limitations

---

# Status

Early research / specification stage.

No production-ready implementation currently exists.
